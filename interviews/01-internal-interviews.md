# Internal Customer Discovery Interviews
> Team members are customers too. They operate agents, use Hub, and build the products.

---

## Interview Template

### Context
- Who are you, what do you do day-to-day?
- What agents do you operate or interact with?

### Problem
- What's the hardest part about working with/between agents?
- When was the last time you wished two agents could talk to each other? What happened?
- What workarounds do you use for agent coordination today?

### Hub-Specific
- How often do you use Hub? For what?
- What works well? What's broken?
- What would make you use Hub 10x more?
- If Hub disappeared tomorrow, what would you lose?

### Combinator-Specific
- Do you think futarchy governance actually works? Evidence?
- Who would pay for it? How much?
- What's the #1 reason a token project would NOT adopt Combinator?

### Pricing
- Would you pay for agent coordination tools? How much?
- What's the most you've seen anyone pay for agent infrastructure?

### Wild Card
- What's the one thing about this startup that you think we're most wrong about?
- If you had to bet the company on ONE product, which one and why?
