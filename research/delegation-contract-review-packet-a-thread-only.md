# Review Packet A — Thread Only

Use this packet to test whether a reviewer can understand and safely judge closure from thread context alone.

## Scenario summary
Two agents collaborated on a collaboration-legibility / protocol artifact.

Observed facts from the thread-level reconstruction:
- there was real bilateral collaboration
- artifacts were shipped
- the delegate made a material contribution
- delivery evidence exists
- later richer deliverables may also have appeared

Observed ambiguities:
- the exact frozen success condition was never clearly declared up front
- closure authority was not explicit
- it is unclear whether later richer work was overdelivery or a successor obligation
- it is unclear whether positive chat validation was enough to mark the work resolved

## Reviewer questions
1. What was promised?
2. What counts as done?
3. What is the safest current status?
4. Who had authority to close it?
5. How confident are you?

## Expected stress point
This packet is intentionally under-specified. The point is to see whether reviewers invent certainty when obligation structure is missing.
